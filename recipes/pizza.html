NY Style Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe will guide you to make the perfect NY Style Pizza.

It will make one 12" pie, enough for a family meal for one night.

Ingredients

  • 1 Jar of Sauce of Choice
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Tablespoon of Sugar
  • Tablespoon of Oregano
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 and 1 half cups low moisture shredded mozerella cheese
  • Toppings of your choosing

Steps

  1. Combine flour,water,sugar,yeast and oregano into large bowl
  2. mix until combined and dough forms
  3. place dough into container and cover with moist cloth,allow to rise 3-4 hours
  4. once dough is risen shape into 12" pie, using flour liberally on surface
  5. pour sauce onto dough,followed by cheese,and toppings of your choice
  6. cook in oven at 550 degrees until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbling
  7. remove pizza from oven, cut into slices, and allow to cool before eating
