Home-Made Tacos Recipe

Description

Quick and easy taco recipe for taco tuesdays at home

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Taco Seasoning
  • 2 Tablespoons Vegetable oil
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cut Chicken into 1/2 inch cubes
  2. Combine chicken and vegetable oil into medium sized pan
  3. Add in taco seasoning and mix together
  4. Cook until very little juice remains in bottom of pan
  5. Heat tortillas in microwave under paper towel and serve
