Sesame Chicken at home

Description

This is THE perfect home-made crispy white meat sesame chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 2lbs Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Garlic Sesame Sauce
  • Jasmine Rice

Steps

  1. Combine eggs,flour,salt,and pepper in large bowl
  2. Cut Chicken into 1 inch cubes
  3. Heat vegetable oil in large pan on medium-high heat
  4. Roll the chicken in the flour mixture and then cook in the vegetable oil until golden brown
  5. Toss finished chicken in sesame sauce
  6. Serve with rice and vegetable of choice
